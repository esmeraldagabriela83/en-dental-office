Dental scaling and brushing

Periodontitis (periodontal disease) is a chronic inflammatory disease.

Like high blood pressure and diabetes, periodontitis is a chronic disease that progresses over time and progressively with discrete symptoms or painful manifestations accompanied by spontaneous bleeding or brushing.

The treatment of periodontitis is complex:

  • elimination of causal factors
  • stop evolving
  • scaling
  • professional brushes
  • immobilization of remaining teeth
  • prosthetic dental works
